👋 Quick start

Onyx Bot rewards you for showing up. Just by chatting, reacting, and RSVPing to events you earn XP and climb the levels. Standout play earns Saga Points. Everything below is available to you as a member — all the slash commands reply privately, so only you see the result.

⌨️ Your commands

📊 /rank YOU

Purpose
See the lifetime-XP leaderboard and your place on it.
How to use
Type /rank for the top 20 (your own row is always pinned at the bottom), or /rank @player to check someone else.
Example
/rank
Result
A private message with your level, total XP, rank, and a progress bar to your next level.
Notes
Only you see the reply. Works in any channel.

🏆 /saga YOU

Purpose
See the Saga Points leaderboard and your standing.
How to use
/saga for the top 10 with your row pinned at the bottom, or /saga @player to check someone.
Example
/saga @Morveril
Result
A private leaderboard of Saga Points. You'll only show up once you've earned some.

❓ /ask TEAM CHANNELS

Purpose
Get answers about what's going on without bugging a leader.
How to use
/ask <your question> in a team channel. It pulls from the announcement threads — latest announcement, current objective, emergencies, or a full recap.
Example
/ask what's the latest announcement?
Result
A private answer based on the posted info. If it doesn't know, it'll tell you to ask a leader rather than make something up.

📋 /roster & /roster-search TEAM CHANNELS

Purpose
View the official roster or check if someone's on it.
How to use
/roster shows the full list; /roster-search <name> finds one player (partial names work).
Example
/roster-search spy
Result
A private roster list, or a ✅/❌ telling you if the player was found.

ℹ️ !commands TEAM CHANNELS

Purpose
A quick reminder of the commands you can use.
How to use
Type !commands in a team channel.
Result
A short, self-deleting list of your slash commands.
⭐ XP & levels

⭐ Earning XP AUTOMATIC

Purpose
Reward you for being active. XP never resets.
How to earn
Chatting: 10 XP per message (max once a minute). Reacting: 5 XP per emoji reaction. RSVPing to an event: 25 XP (once per event).
Leveling
XP builds toward levels — quick early on, much harder past level 25 and again past 100.
Result
When you level up, the bot announces it right in the channel. Check your progress anytime with /rank.
Notes
The leaderboard channels themselves don't give XP.
🏆 Saga Points

🏆 The prestige track PARTICIPATE

Purpose
Recognize standout in-game performance — the community's prestige ranking.
How to earn
Play well in operations (pathing, siege MVP, final hit, and other objectives). Post your proof in the claims channel — confirmed achievements are added to your total.
Season rewards
Saga resets each season. When it does, the top 10 earn lifetime-XP bonuses (the bigger your finish, the bigger the bonus).
Result
Your points show on the team leaderboard and via /saga.
Notes
Points are earned through real achievement — keep showing up and making plays.
📅 Events & RSVP

⏰ Event reminders & RSVP EVERYONE

Purpose
Make sure you never miss a scheduled op, and show whether you're coming.
How to use
About an hour before a scheduled event, the bot posts a reminder with ✅ In, ❌ Out, and ❔ Maybe buttons. Tap one to RSVP — tap a different one anytime to change your answer.
Example
Tap ✅ In on the reminder post
Result
The post updates live with who's In / Out / Maybe, and you earn 25 XP for RSVPing (once per event).
Notes
No command needed — just watch for the reminder and tap a button.
🏅 Leaderboards

🏅 Live leaderboard channels VIEW

Purpose
See the standings any time without running a command.
How to use
Open the team leaderboard channel (Saga Points + Lifetime XP) or the chill-zone leaderboard (Lifetime XP).
Result
A pinned-style message the bot keeps updated automatically (refreshes every few minutes).
Notes
Want your personal rank with a progress bar? Use /rank or /saga.
